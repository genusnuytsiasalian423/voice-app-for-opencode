## 📖 About this application

This application turns written text into spoken audio. It runs on your Windows computer. The software uses high-quality voices to read your documents or notes aloud. You can control the speed, volume, and playback of the generated speech. The interface includes simple buttons for common tasks. You do not need to know how to write code to use this tool.

This application works on most Windows computers. Ensure your computer meets these basic specifications to enjoy a smooth experience.

* A stable internet connection for the first-time setup process.
* At least 200 megabytes of free space on your hard drive.
* Speakers or headphones connected to your device.

## 🚀 Getting started

Once you install the app, look for the shortcut on your desktop. Double-click the icon to open the main window. 

The main window shows a text box. You can type or paste the text you want the app to read. Choose a voice from the drop-down menu. Press the Play button to hear the audio. Use the Pause button to stop the playback. Use the slider to change the volume of the output. 

## ⚙️ How to use the settings

The application provides a settings menu to manage your voice preferences. Click the gear icon in the top corner to open these options.

* Voice Selection: Pick between different male and female voices.
* Speed Control: Adjust the rate of speech. Move the slider to the right for faster speech and to the left for slower speech.
* Output Folder: Choose where the app saves your audio files when you export them.
* Default Settings: Reset the app to its original state if you change too many options.

## 💡 Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems, check these solutions first.

1. App does not open: Ensure you installed the latest version. Try restarting your computer.
2. No sound: Check your Windows volume levels. Verify that your speakers connect correctly to your computer. Select a different audio output device in your Windows sound settings.
3. Voice sounds robotic: Check that your internet connection works. The app requires a connection in the background to fetch voice data from the server.
4. Interface is slow: Close other programs that consume a lot of system memory.
